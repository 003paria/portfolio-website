About Us

Shiraz Café is an Iranian café and tea house, covered by a cozy and oriental atmosphere based in Montreal since 1992.
The café specializes in herbal teas, tisanes, cold drinks, and pastries. There is a wide variety of tea section for customers to make their own blends.
Among our range of teas, Persian Fog is the most popular. We also serve breakfast, sandwiches, and salads
