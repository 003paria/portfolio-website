About Us

Shiraz Café

Shiraz Café is an Iranian café and tea house, covered by a cozy and oriental atmosphere based in Montreal since 1992. The café specializes in herbal teas, tisanes, cold drinks, and pastries-seven days a week. There is a wide variety of tea section for customers to make their own blends. Among our range of teas, Persian Fog is the most popular. We also serve breakfast, sandwiches, and salads. Our space is ideal for your daily caffeine fix or your Happy Hour gatherings.
